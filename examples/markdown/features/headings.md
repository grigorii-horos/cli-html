# Headings

Markdown supports six levels of headings.

# Heading 1

This is the largest heading, typically used for page titles.

## Heading 2

Used for major sections.

### Heading 3

Used for subsections.

#### Heading 4

Used for sub-subsections.

##### Heading 5

Less commonly used, but available.

###### Heading 6

The smallest heading level.
